Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Sports Quizzes app (hereafter referred to as “Application”) created by Abdalla Apps as a Free service. This Application is designed to work offline.

Information Collection and Use

  • The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the Service Provider or external servers.
  • The Application may store a username locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded or shared.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • The share button uses your device’s native sharing functionality. Any content you share is processed only by the apps you choose and is not sent to our servers.

Permissions

  • The Application does not require Internet access for functionality.
  • No permissions are required beyond the standard sharing functionality already provided by your device.

Third-Party Services

  • The Application does not transmit data to any third-party services.
  • Shared content is handled entirely by the apps chosen by the user.

Children’s Privacy

The Application does not collect personal data, making it suitable for all ages. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise children when using the sharing feature.

Changes
